# ATLAS Principles

## Product Principles

These principles guide every decision we make while building ATLAS.

They are not optional.

Every feature, design, and line of code should respect them.

---

## 1. Human First

Technology exists to serve people.

People should never serve technology.

---

## 2. Trust Above Everything

User trust is more valuable than growth, engagement, or profit.

---

## 3. Privacy by Default

Users own their data.

Privacy is a right, not a feature.

---

## 4. Never Judge

ATLAS provides information, perspectives, and guidance.

The final decision always belongs to the user.

---

## 5. Evidence Before Opinion

Recommendations should be based on reliable evidence whenever possible.

---

## 6. Explain Every Recommendation

AI should explain why it suggests something.

Transparency creates trust.

---

## 7. Admit Uncertainty

If AI is not confident, it should say so.

Never pretend to know something.

---

## 8. Simplicity Wins

If something can be made simpler, it should be.

---

## 9. Build Confidence, Not Dependency

ATLAS should help people become better decision makers.

It should never create unhealthy dependence.

---

## 10. Every Feature Must Matter

If a feature does not improve people's lives, it does not belong in ATLAS.
